Beginners Guide

Step-by-step

1
Create a Coinbase account
Download the Coinbase app and start the sign up process. You will need a valid ID and may be asked for proof of address in order to transact, so be sure to have those ready. Verifying your ID may take longer than a few minutes, depending on where you live.
2
Add a payment method
Tap on the payment method box and connect a payment method. You can use a bank account, debit card or initiate a wire.
3
Start a trade
On Coinbase.com, select Buy & Sell. On the Coinbase mobile app, tap the ( + ) Buy on the Home tab.
4
Select Ethereum from list of assets
On Coinbase.com, click the Buy panel to search and select Ethereum. On the Coinbase mobile app, search for Ethereum by typing “Ethereum” into the search bar. When you see Ethereum appear in the results, tap it to open up the purchase screen.
5
Enter the amount you want to buy
Use the number pad to input the amount you want to spend in your local currency. The app will automatically convert that into a Ethereum amount. You can tap the arrow buttons to the right of the amount of money you want to spend to switch the number to the equivalent amount of Ethereum
6
Finalize your purchase of Ethereum
Tap “Preview buy” when ready. You’ll be shown the details of your purchase. Make sure everything looks good and then confirm your purchase by clicking “Buy now”.
7
Done
Once the order processes, you’ll be taken to the confirmation screen. That’s it, you’ve bought Ethereum. It may take a few days before Coinbase allows you to send this out of their exchange. Depending on the amount sometimes it may take a week. If so come back next week and move to step 2!!

Moving out of Coinbase

Now it's time to transfer your ETH out of Coinbase and into your newly created MetaMask wallet. What you’ll need for this step is the wallet address from MetaMask. The video below outlines exactly how to do this.

  1. Login to coinbase click the "Transfer" button at the top right.
  2. Select the first option "Send Crypto".
  3. You can then specify the amount you would like to send. Switch the view to ETH to send a specific amount of ETH such as 0.5 ETH. Optionally you can specify the USD equivalent amount of ETH to send.
  4. Next click "To: Network and Recipient". Click "Ethereum" as that is the network we will be sending the crypto on. Now you are ready to paste in the address of your MetaMask wallet.
  5. Click the fox icon in your browser to open your MetaMask wallet. At the top there is a small pane where it shows some of the first and last characters of your wallet address "0xAKSK....90991" as an example. Click on this and it will copy it to your clipboard.
  6. In coinbase paste your copied address in the search field at the top entitled "Ethereum address or ENS" and hit Enter.
  7. Click Preview Send. Here you will see an overview of what will happen. How much ETH you are sending, how much ETH will be spent as a transaction fee (gas fee), and to whom you are sending. Please verify it is your wallet! The gas fees may be high during times of high usuage on the network. Soon we will be switching to a new network, PulseChain, where the gas fees are more like one to two cents.
  8. If everything looks good, send your ETH! It may take some time to send over, Coinbase shows the estimated amount of time for the transaction to be written to the blockchain. After this the ETH will show up in your MetaMask Wallet!!
  9. CONGRATS!! At this point you have crypto which you actually OWN the KEYS for!!
  10. Now lets work on getting to a more efficient network and getting Nachos to celebrate!

Now we are going to move from the Ethereum Network to the PulseChain Network

The PulseChain network has substantially cheaper gas/transaction fees and is the home of the Nacho Token!

  1. We are going to use a service called UniSwap to make the switch. (There are many other ways to do this) Open UniSwap.
  2. Click on Connect Wallet. Select the MetaMask Icon. Click Next in the window. Then click Connect. Your wallet is now connected and we are going to make the swap.
  3. Note: Don't swap all of the ETH because you will need some ETH later on for gas fees if you swap back to the ETH network. We leave about $50 worth of ETH in there.
  4. Click on Select Token. In the search bar paste in the WPLS token address. The address is 0xA882606494D86804B5514E07e6Bd2D6a6eE6d68A.
  5. Click on "Wrapped Pulse from PulseChain" item that pops up. Next click "I Understand" to acknowledge we are leaving Centralized Crypto and moving into the decentralized world. Welcome to DeFi!
  6. Now choose your amount of ETH to swap, remembering to leave some gas in the tank.
  7. When you are ready, hit Swap. It will then ask you to review and confirm the swap. Then MetaMask will pop up, scroll down to click the blue Confirm Button.
  8. After a few moments UniSwap will inform you that the swap was succesful. In order to view the WPLS in your MetaMask wallet on the Ethereum network, we will need to add the token to MetaMask.
  9. Click the fox to open MetaMask, Select the Tokens tab, and then click the link "+ Import Tokens". At the top Select "Custom token". Here you will paste the token address for WPLS from above and click Next. Then click Import. Now you can see your WPLS balance in MetaMask on the Ethereum Network.
  10. Now you are ready to bridge these tokens to the PulseChain Network.

Next Add PulseChain network to MetaMask Wallet

So far we have only been operating on the Ethereum network but soon we will be briding some funds over to the PulseChain Network. This quick step will allow you to get familar with networks in MetaMask and be able to switch back and forth between Ethereum and PulseChain.

  1. Visit http://pulsechain.com and click the big button that says "Add PulseChain to MetaMask.
  2. Now it will prompt you to switch networks. Click Switch Network. You can easily switch networks in MetaMask by clicking the Fox, going to the very top left, and clicking the dropdown.
  3. Click Approve in MetaMask. Next step is to bridge over the WPLS from the Ethereum side to the PulseChain side.

Or Watch this 2 minute video to get comfortable.

Now you have crypto on the Ethereum network, the fees are high there so its time to get over to a better network with low fees. Lets move over to PulseChain. Head over to the bridge by visiting https://bridge.pulsechain.com. Click the white button "Bridge.mypinata.cloud".

  1. Click the Connect Your Wallet Button. Then click the MetaMask Icon. MetaMask will popup and require you to click Next.
  2. Now you can click the pink "MAX" text in the bridge window to autofill in all of the WPLS tokens you now have. Or just enter in how many WPLS tokens you want to send the the PulseChain.
  3. Leave everything else the same, and click Send. Then click the Send button again. MetaMask wil pop up to require you to Confirm. It may take about 30 minutes to bridge over. Don't panic, be patient!
  4. After this is complete your Wrapped Pulse (WPLS) will arrive on the PulseChain network in your wallet as Unwrapped Pulse (PLS). PLS is the native token for PulseChain and will be what is used for gas on the network. Transaction fees now in 2024 are only 1-2 cents each!! Sure beats the huge ETH fees. Now you will be able to swap your PLS for ANY token on PulseChain.
  5. Once its done, change your network in MetaMask to PulseChain to see all of your PLS! Get ready for Nachos!!!
